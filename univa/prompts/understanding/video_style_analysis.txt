## Prompt for Generating New Videos Based on Reference Videos (Applicable to Qwen2-VL and Other Multimodal Models)

**Task Description:**
Please analyze the provided reference video segment and generate an analysis report based on it.

**Generation Requirements:**
1. **Visual Style:** Capture and reproduce the overall color tone, lighting, and visual texture of the reference video (e.g., cinematic, anime style, vlog style).
2. **Camera Language:** Mimic the camera movements in the reference video, such as camera angles, movement trajectories (push, pull, dolly, truck, follow), shot sizes (long shot, full shot, medium shot, close-up, extreme close-up), and editing pace.
3. **Plot (Optional):** If the reference video contains a clear storyline, generate a new story segment with similar plot developments, turning points, or themes.
4. **Scenes:** Recreate the distinctive settings or overall atmosphere of the scenes in the reference video (e.g., serene natural scenery, bustling city night scenes, tense suspenseful atmosphere).

**Output Requirements:**

Output strictly in JSON format.
{
    "visual_style": ".......",
    "camera_language": "......",
    "plot": "......",
    "scene": "......"
}
